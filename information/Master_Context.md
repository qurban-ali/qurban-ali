## Project: SmartMatch
SmartMatch is an AI-powered recruitment and talent intelligence platform developed specifically for the aviation industry. The platform enables recruiters to manage a centralized talent pool, process candidate CVs, and identify the most relevant candidates based on job descriptions, skills, experience, qualifications, and recruiter-provided keywords.

I worked on the full-stack development of the application using React.js for the frontend and Node.js for the backend. The platform uses MySQL for structured candidate, recruiter, and application data, while Apache Solr supports high-performance search, indexing, filtering, and vector-based candidate retrieval.

The system parses uploaded CVs and transforms candidate information into structured data, including professional experience, technical skills, certifications, education, aviation roles, and other relevant details. AI-generated embeddings are indexed in Solr, allowing semantic searches rather than relying only on exact keyword matching.

When a recruiter enters a job description or search criteria, SmartMatch compares the requirements with candidate profiles, retrieves suitable candidates, and generates an AI-based matching score. The platform also supports talent-pool management, advanced filtering, application tracking, and recruiter collaboration.

AI integrations may include Google Gemini for CV parsing and structured information extraction, together with OpenAI embedding and language models for semantic retrieval, ranking, scoring, and explanations. ## Project: TalentVista
TalentVista is an AI-powered talent acquisition platform designed to help recruiters process large volumes of applications, build reusable talent pools, and identify the most relevant candidates for each job opportunity.

Recruiters can upload candidate CVs from job boards, professional networks, ATS platforms, or internal databases together with a job description. The system parses both CVs and JDs, extracting structured details such as skills, experience, education, certifications, seniority, job roles, and industry expertise.

Candidate and job data are converted into vector embeddings and indexed in Apache Solr, enabling semantic search, keyword matching, advanced filtering, and AI-assisted retrieval. Each candidate is evaluated against the job requirements, assigned a relevance score, and ranked for recruiter review, with matching strengths and potential gaps highlighted.

The platform allows recruiters to maintain categorized talent pools by role, department, industry, and skills. For future vacancies, they can upload a new JD and search their existing pool instead of restarting the sourcing process.

TalentVista supports shortlisting, multi-stage interviews, interviewer feedback, scorecards, and candidate comparisons. It can generate screening and interview questions based on the JD, candidate profile, required skills, and interview stage. Scores and feedback from HR, technical, and management rounds are consolidated to support consistent, data-informed hiring decisions.

I contributed to the full-stack development and AI architecture using React.js, Node.js, MySQL, Apache Solr, REST APIs, vector embeddings, semantic search, and multiple LLM integrations for CV/JD parsing, structured extraction, candidate matching, ranking, scoring explanations, interview-question generation, and recruitment reporting.

## Project: HumanJot
HumanJot is an AI-powered content creation and writing-assistance platform designed for marketers, creators, agencies, entrepreneurs, and professionals who need high-quality content for multiple digital channels.

Users can select a content type or social platform, provide a topic, brief, campaign idea, or draft, and generate tailored content for LinkedIn, X/Twitter, blogs, marketing pages, and other social channels. The platform adapts the structure, tone, length, formatting, call to action, and hashtags to suit the selected platform instead of producing the same generic copy everywhere.

A key capability is personalized content generation. Users can brainstorm ideas, generate complete drafts, refine existing text, rewrite sections, and request improvements until the content matches their needs.

The multi-platform workflow allows users to prepare one core message and generate optimized variations for several channels at once. Each version is adapted to the conventions and audience of its platform, helping users maintain a consistent campaign while reducing the time required to rewrite content manually.

The solution supports content ideation, social posts, long-form articles, blogs, scripts, promotional copy, captions, campaign messaging, editing, and research-assisted writing.

## Project: TalentVista
TalentVista is an AI-powered talent acquisition platform designed to help recruiters process large volumes of applications, build reusable talent pools, and identify the most relevant candidates for each job opportunity.

Recruiters can upload candidate CVs from job boards, professional networks, ATS platforms, or internal databases together with a job description. The system parses both CVs and JDs, extracting structured details such as skills, experience, education, certifications, seniority, job roles, and industry expertise.

Candidate and job data are converted into vector embeddings and indexed in Apache Solr, enabling semantic search, keyword matching, advanced filtering, and AI-assisted retrieval. Each candidate is evaluated against the job requirements, assigned a relevance score, and ranked for recruiter review, with matching strengths and potential gaps highlighted.

The platform allows recruiters to maintain categorized talent pools by role, department, industry, and skills. For future vacancies, they can upload a new JD and search their existing pool instead of restarting the sourcing process.

TalentVista supports shortlisting, multi-stage interviews, interviewer feedback, scorecards, and candidate comparisons. It can generate screening and interview questions based on the JD, candidate profile, required skills, and interview stage. Scores and feedback from HR, technical, and management rounds are consolidated to support consistent, data-informed hiring decisions.

I contributed to the full-stack development and AI architecture using React.js, Node.js, MySQL, Apache Solr, REST APIs, vector embeddings, semantic search, and multiple LLM integrations for CV/JD parsing, structured extraction, candidate matching, ranking, scoring explanations, interview-question generation, and recruitment reporting.
